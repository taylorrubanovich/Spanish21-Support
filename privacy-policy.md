# Privacy Policy for Spanish21 Strategy

**Effective date:** May 8, 2026

Spanish21 Strategy ("the App") is a mobile application that provides Spanish 21 basic strategy training and gameplay simulation.

[Support Home](./index.html)

This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and what choices users have.

## Information We Collect

At this time, the App does not require account creation and does not collect any personal information.

The App may store limited app data locally on your device, such as:

- game setup selections
- in-game preference settings

This local data is used only to improve your experience in the App, for example by remembering your preferred rules and display settings between sessions.

## How Information Is Used

Any locally stored app data is used only to:

- save your selected table rules and play preferences
- restore your settings the next time you open the App
- support normal gameplay and app functionality

## Data Sharing

The App does not sell your personal information.

The App does not share personal information with third parties because the App does not currently collect personal information from users.

## Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

At this time, the App does not use:

- third-party advertising SDKs
- third-party analytics SDKs
- cross-app tracking technologies

If this changes in a future version, this Privacy Policy will be updated before or at the time the change takes effect.

## Data Retention

Any settings saved by the App are stored locally on your device and remain there until:

- you uninstall the App
- you clear the App's storage through your device settings
- a future version of the App changes how settings are stored

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and the App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Security

The App uses the storage and security features provided by the operating system for locally saved settings. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

## Your Choices

Because the App currently stores only limited local settings data, you can remove that data by:

- uninstalling the App
- clearing the App's storage from your device settings

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. When updates are made, the new version will be posted at the same URL and the effective date above will be revised.
