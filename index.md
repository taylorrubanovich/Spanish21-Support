## FAQ

### What is Spanish21 Strategy?

Spanish21 Strategy is a mobile training app designed to help players practice correct Spanish 21 basic strategy decisions under different table-rule configurations.

### Does the app use real money or offer gambling?

No. Spanish21 Strategy is a practice and training app only. It does not offer real-money gambling.

### Which rules can I practice?

The app supports multiple Spanish 21 rule variations, including dealer soft 17 settings, deck counts, surrender and forfeit behavior, and maximum doubling options.

### Does the app save my settings?

Yes. The app may save selected table rules and in-game preferences locally on your device so your setup is remembered between sessions.

### Does the app collect personal data?

At this time, the app does not require an account and does not intentionally collect personal information. For details, see the [Privacy Policy](./privacy-policy.html).

### How do I report a strategy discrepancy or bug?

Please email [taylorrubanovich@gmail.com](mailto:taylorrubanovich@gmail.com?subject=Spanish21%20Strategy%20Bug%20Report) with the issue details, and include screenshots or step-by-step reproduction notes if possible.

### How do I remove locally saved settings?

You can remove locally stored app settings by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's storage from your device settings.
